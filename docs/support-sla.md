# Support & SLA

This document summarizes Aurion Labs' public support packages and service-level expectations.

## Support Packages

### Basic Support
- Response time: 48–72 hours
- Scope: Minor fixes only
- Exclusions: No structural changes or feature builds

### Managed Support
- Response time: 24–48 hours
- Scope: Content edits, monitoring, small workflow improvements
- Includes: Monthly check-ins and performance notes

### Infrastructure Support
- Response time: priority queue
- Scope: Workflow troubleshooting, automation help, dashboard and operational support
- Includes: deeper technical assistance under a separate agreement

## Support Rules
- Working hours and emergency definitions are agreed during onboarding.
- Emergency support is separately contracted and defined.
- Revision caps apply; major workflow or integration changes require a separate quote.
- Delayed client feedback may affect delivery timelines.

## How to Request Support
- Book a short support call via the website contact page
- Use WhatsApp for quick triage and prioritization

## Notes
These packages are designed to keep post-launch work predictable and protect operational bandwidth. For enterprise-style SLAs, we provide custom agreements.
